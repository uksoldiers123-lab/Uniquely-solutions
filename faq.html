Payers

Do I need an account to make a payment?

No. You can pay as a guest on the Make a Payment page. We’ll email a receipt to the address you provide.

Make a payment

Which payment methods are accepted?

All major credit and debit cards. Many merchants also accept bank payments (ACH). Availability may vary by merchant.

How long do ACH payments take?

ACH typically takes 3–5 business days to clear. Your bank may show a pending debit before final settlement.

Will I be charged a fee to pay?

Most payments do not include a payer fee. If a merchant applies a convenience or surcharge where permitted by law, it will be clearly shown before you submit.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are issued by the merchant. Card refunds typically post in 5–10 business days. ACH refunds can take 3–5 business days after approval.

I didn’t get a receipt. What should I do?

Check your spam folder and verify the email you entered. If you still can’t find it, contact the merchant or reach out to support at support@uniquitysolutions.com.

My payment was declined. Why?

Common reasons include incorrect card details, insufficient funds, bank security checks, or AVS ZIP mismatch. Try again or contact your bank. If issues persist, contact support.

Merchants

How do I open a merchant account?

Complete our short application. A specialist will review and follow up if additional information is required.

Create merchant account

How fast can I start accepting payments?

Many businesses are provisionally approved the same day. Production gateway credentials are issued after underwriting is complete.

Do you support in‑person payments?

Yes. We offer compatible terminals and POS integrations. See Integrations or contact Sales for hardware options.

Can I set up subscriptions or recurring billing?

Yes. Recurring billing is supported via dashboard and API with automatic retries and dunning options.

How are chargebacks handled?

We notify you of disputes, provide evidence submission tools, and track outcomes. Best‑practice guides are available to reduce dispute rates.

What settlement schedules do you offer?

Standard card settlement is T+2 business days in the U.S. ACH funding typically arrives T+2 to T+3. Options may vary by risk profile and region.

Which countries and currencies are supported?

Primary operations are U.S. and Canada with USD and CAD settlement. Contact Sales for cross‑border options.

Pricing & billing

How is pricing structured?

Simple, transparent processing rates with optional add‑ons for recurring billing, advanced fraud tools, and in‑person hardware. See our pricing page for details.

View pricing

Are there setup or monthly fees?

No setup fees. Monthly fees may apply for premium features or terminals if selected.

How do I access statements and reports?

Statements and deposits are available in your dashboard. You can export settlements, fees, and payouts in CSV format.

Do you issue 1099‑K forms?

Yes, when required by law. Forms are delivered electronically in the dashboard and via email notifications.

Developers

How do I get API keys?

After your merchant account is created, you can generate test and live API keys in the dashboard. Keep live keys secure and do not embed them in client‑side code.

Do you have a sandbox?

Yes. Use test API keys and sample cards/ACH tokens to simulate transactions without real charges.

How do webhooks work?

Register a webhook endpoint in the dashboard. We sign events; verify signatures and return 2xx to acknowledge. Retry with exponential backoff is supported.

Are SDKs available?

Server SDKs for Node, Python, Ruby, and PHP. Client libraries are available for tokenized elements and hosted fields.

Developers & API

What are the rate limits?

Reasonable per‑key rate limits apply to protect service integrity. Contact Support if you anticipate sustained high throughput.

How do you handle API versioning?

We use dated versions with backward‑compatible changes where possible. Pin a version via request header. Deprecations include advance notice.

Security & compliance

Are you PCI compliant?

Yes. We maintain PCI DSS Level 1 compliance. Use hosted fields / tokenization to minimize your PCI scope.

Compliance

Do you store card or bank numbers?

We tokenize sensitive data and transmit over encrypted channels (TLS 1.2+). Raw PANs and bank numbers are not stored in a way accessible to merchants.

How do I report a security issue?

Email our security team at security@uniquitysolutions.com. Include steps to reproduce and affected systems if possible.

Where can I find your privacy and terms?

See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
